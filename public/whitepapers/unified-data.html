Unified Data for AI: The Data Foundation Your Agentic Strategy Cannot Ignore

Unified data is the first layer of AI maturity for financial institutions.

1. Faster Data Access

Trusted data available through APIs, workbenches, query layers, and reusable data products.

2. Better Data Quality

Duplicate records, stale feeds, schema changes, and missing values are detected before they affect decisions.

3. Stronger Governance

Lineage, ownership, access control, refresh logic, and auditability are built into the data foundation.

4. Reusable Data Products

The same trusted data supports dashboards, analytics, AI services, agents, workﬂows, and reports.

5. Lower Cost and Higher Scale

Compute, query performance, refresh cycles, and cloud usage are monitored and optimised.

From Data Foundation to Enterprise Workbenches Uniﬁed data powers every enterprise workbench in the BERG platform. CIO, CRO, Compliance, Operations, Audit, RM, and CXO workbenches all depend on the same trusted foundation. They use the same ontology. They consume the same AI services. They work from the same version of truth.

Uniﬁed Data Fabric -> Decision Intelligence -> Agentic Workﬂows -> Enterprise Workbenches

Workbench Examples

CIO Workbench: investment signals, portfolio impact, CIO memos

CRO Workbench: risk indicators, breaches, evidence, remediation

Compliance Workbench: obligations, controls, evidence, regulatory actions

Operations Workbench: exceptions, root cause, SLA, closure

Audit Workbench: evidence, ﬁndings, remediation, reporting

Final Thought

AI strategy cannot succeed on fragmented data. Before institutions deploy agents, build workbenches, or automate workﬂows, they need a trusted data foundation that connects systems, documents, rules, signals, evidence, and decisions. Uniﬁed data gives ontology something reliable to model, analytics something consistent to calculate, agents something trustworthy to reason on, workﬂows something auditable to act on, and business teams one operating layer to monitor, decide, and act.

In the age of agentic AI, uniﬁed data is not an IT asset. It is the foundation of decision intelligence.
